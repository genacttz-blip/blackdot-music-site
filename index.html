About Us

BlackDot Music is the evolution of BlackDot Music — a Dar es Salaam–born studio that has produced records for leading artists across East Africa. Building on years of studio excellence, we now operate as a full-service creative agency that connects music, media, brand storytelling, talent management, and live experiences.

Mission

Discover and develop talent, build powerful IP, and deliver world-class creative solutions for artists, brands, and communities.

Vision

To be Africa’s most culturally relevant creative company — shaping sound, visuals and experiences that move people and markets.

Our Mandate & Divisions

Talent Management

  • Roster Development: scouting, A&R, image & brand building, media training.
  • Career Management: strategy, release planning, show bookings, touring.
  • Commercial Deals: endorsements, sponsorships, influencer campaigns.
  • Artist Services: PRO registrations, split sheets, neighboring rights, statements.

Publishing Administration

  • IP & Metadata: ISRC/ISWC, cue sheets, catalog database, splits & disputes.
  • Registration & Collection: COSOTA/PROs, publishing portals, YouTube CMS.
  • Licensing & Sync: ads, film/TV, games, corporate — negotiation & clearance.
  • Royalty Accounting: ingestion, matching, statements, audits.

Creative & Media Design

  • Audio: music production, mixing & mastering, sonic branding, jingles, podcasts, voice‑over, sound design.
  • Video: music videos, commercials, documentaries, motion graphics, short‑form and live production.
  • Design: art direction, campaign key visuals, album covers, social media kits.
  • Studios: multi-room audio suites and a compact content stage for agile shoots.

Event Management

  • Concept & Curation: show formats, lineups, creative direction.
  • Production: stage, sound, light, broadcast, vendor management.
  • Sponsorships: packages, proposals, ROI reporting, brand integrations.
  • Touring & Logistics: permits, riders, settlements, reconciliations.
