About Us

BlackDot Music is the evolution of BlackDot Music Studio — a Dar es Salaam–born studio that has produced records for leading artists across East Africa. Building on years of studio excellence, we now operate as a full-service creative agency that connects music, media, brand storytelling, talent management, and live experiences.

Vision: To be Africa’s most culturally relevant creative company — shaping sound, visuals and experiences that move people and markets.

Mission: Discover and develop talent, build powerful IP, and deliver world‑class creative solutions for artists, brands, and communities.
